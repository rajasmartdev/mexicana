The Spirit of Mexico in Williamstown

Nestled inside the historic Rose of Australia Hotel, Mexicana @ Rose brings the vibrant flavours and warm hospitality of Mexico to Ferguson Street. Every dish we serve is a celebration of authentic recipes passed down through generations, prepared fresh each day with quality ingredients and genuine passion.

  • Hand-pressed tortillas made fresh every morning
  • House-made salsas and guacamole crafted from scratch
  • Traditional recipes with authentic Mexican spices and techniques

From our famous elote (Mexican grilled corn) to perfectly seasoned carne asada, sizzling fajitas to sweet churros, every bite transports you to the heart of Mexico. Our commitment to authenticity means no shortcuts, no artificial mixes – just real Mexican food the way it's meant to be enjoyed. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, celebrating with friends in our beer garden, or enjoying unlimited tacos on Wednesday nights, Mexicana @ Rose is where great food meets genuine connection. We're not just serving meals; we're creating experiences and building a community, one taco at a time.
